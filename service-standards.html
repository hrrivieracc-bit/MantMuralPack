Service Guidelines
The Member-Pleasing Business
Excellent service is the name of our game.
Accommodate
  • Address members by their last name.
  • Do everything in your power to make a member happy.
  • Can't help? Find someone who can.
Attitude
  • Be friendly and welcoming, in person and on the phone.
  • Never discuss personal problems with members.
  • Never complain about your job in front of a member.
Appearance
  • Neat, clean uniform and good personal hygiene.
  • No gum, eating, drinking or smoking while working.
  • Look sharp — that pride reaches every member.
