Uniform & Grooming Standards
Every team member, every shift
Always in Uniform
Uniform
Clean, pressed, no rips or stains. Plain white undershirt, not visible at the neck. Tucked in, belt with belt loops, pants at the waist.
Name Badge
Worn at all times — it is part of your attire. Never deface it or add stickers.
Shoes
Clean & polished; slip-resistant or safety shoes where required. No sandals, open-back, or clogs (except kitchen).
Grooming
Hair clean & natural; food handlers wear a net. Facial hair neatly trimmed. Tattoos covered.
Hygiene & Nails
Shower daily, use deodorant, fragrance in moderation. Nails neat; F&B staff keep them short with no polish.
Phones
No phone while working — breaks only. Smart watches are OK, but no calls on duty.

Report in full uniform for every shift. Team members who are inappropriately dressed will be sent home to change.  ·  Uniforms are provided at no cost and returned clean upon separation.
